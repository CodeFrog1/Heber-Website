Welcome to Heber City!

Nothing but a taste of our glourous city



Ever since Heber was founded in July of 1859 new poeple, new buildings, new businesses and new activities have flooded Heber City, up and till today. Here I will tell you about these things and more including new developments in commerce.

In 1850 sawmill workers in Big cottonwomm Canyon hiked to the summit of the Wasatch Range and saw Heber Valley. Thus in 1855, a settlement was established directing new immigrants to the new land daily. This settlement was a mormon community and with some help from the newly arived immigrants the Provo Canyon Road Company was established, creating a road up Provo Canyon and through what is now the Heber Valley. This road would save immigrants, traders, and the military time and money.

Since 1899 to 1915 the Heber City economy depended on agriculture, livestock, and dairying. Back then the Rio Grande Western railway track became the shipping center for agricultural products. As Heber grew during 1920 to 1990 local businesses grew such as hotels, retail stores, markets, lumberyards, banks, and even non-local businesses such as restaurant chains.

New activities in Heber ever since the 1990s include riding the Heber Valley railroad all the up to provo while listening to country music and learning about Heber Valley history and legends. You can also hike the Unita National Forest with its breathtaking geography and views draw in people from all ages who mountain bike, ski, hike and camp. You can visit Deer Creek State Park with its exapnisve lake with many oportunities for fishing, swimming, boating and kayak. You can also visit the CAF Utah Wing Museum in Heber and see there many collections of airplanes over the decades as well as their history and use in those eras.
