Heber's Wonderous Mountains

If there is one thing in Heber you should see, it is Timpanogos. As the biggest mountain in Wasatch county, as the mountain with
the most activities and legends behind it, it should be your first choice when chossing a place to see in Heber. As the biggest mountain
in Wasatch county is has the most breathtaking overlook when you hike to the top and it has a cave you can explore once you get there.
