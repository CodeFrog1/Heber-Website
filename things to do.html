Heber's Glorious trails

Dutch Hollow is one of many biking and hiking trails you can do in Heber. Located on river road to the left of the light going towards
Park City, Dutch Hollow has a variety of trails from long and treacherous to short and easy. For those who want a day of exercise and for those
who want to see the view at the top of these hills then Dutch Hollow is the place for you.
